How will mobility work in the future in Cologne?

How can the city of cologne be supplied with energy in the future?

How many bike lanes does cologne need?

Science in Dialogue with Siemens, the City of Cologne and the TH Cologne will bring researchers, city planners, coders and citizens together in labs.

The start will be a two-day hackathon. At the hackathon first prototypes and ideas will be developed. To further develop the prototypes we will start new working groups that will meet on a regular basis.
